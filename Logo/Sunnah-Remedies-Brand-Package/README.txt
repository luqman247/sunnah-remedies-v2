The mark is a faithful vector trace of your supplied artwork — proportions,
strokes and internal relationships are unchanged.

CHOOSING A VARIATION
  Emerald / dark background ....... icon-primary  OR  icon-ivory-reversed
  Light / ivory background ........ icon-emerald  OR  icon-emerald-gold
  App icon / avatar ............... icon-app-rounded  (favicon-16/32/48 for chrome)
  Single-ink ...................... icon-mono-black / icon-mono-white
  With the name ................... lockup-horizontal-*  /  lockup-stacked-*
                                    (-descriptor adds "Institute of Prophetic Medicine")

COLOUR
  Deep Emerald  #0E3B2E    Antique Gold  #C7A25A    Warm Ivory  #F6F3EE

TYPE
  Libre Baskerville (wordmark, headings)   ·   Inter (descriptor, body, labels)

Wordmarks in the lockups are converted to outlines, so the SVGs render
identically without the fonts installed.
