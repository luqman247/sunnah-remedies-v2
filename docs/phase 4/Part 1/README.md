# Sunnah Remedies — Institutional Operations Handbook

**The internal operating manual of the headquarters.** Version 1.0 · Classification: Internal
Custodian: Office of the Chief Experience Officer

The handbook every employee, developer, editor, photographer, practitioner, and teacher uses. It sits beneath the strategic *Operational Blueprint* (the prior phase) and translates it into the daily reality of a physical institution — the rooms, the people, the safety, the SOPs.

## The order of authority (memorise)
1. Law & regulatory obligations
2. Safety & safeguarding
3. The Operational Blueprint (the constitution)
4. This Handbook
5. Local team practice

Higher number yields to lower. When in doubt, stop and escalate (Ch 12).

## The one idea
**Two ledgers, one standard.** Every decision is judged commercially *and* by scholarly/clinical integrity — and the Integrity Ledger holds veto. That single asymmetry is what makes this an institution, not a shop.

---

## How this relates to the Blueprint
This Handbook is the **human and physical layer**; the Blueprint is the **strategic and digital layer**. Where they meet, the Handbook points to the Blueprint rather than repeating it:

*A note on scope: this Handbook flags where regulated matters arise — health-and-safety, clinical governance, special-treatment/cupping licensing, food safety, data protection, safeguarding, insurance, employment, advertising/labelling — but it is an operational handbook, not legal, clinical, or professional advice. The institution retains qualified counsel in each area, whose guidance is binding and governs where it differs from this text.*
