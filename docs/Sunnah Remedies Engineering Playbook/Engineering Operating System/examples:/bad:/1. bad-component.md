# Bad Example — ProductCard

## Responsibilities

Displays UI.

Fetches products.

Handles checkout.

Processes payments.

Updates user profile.

Manages authentication.

Runs search.

Creates reviews.

Handles analytics.

---

## Problems

❌ Multiple responsibilities.

❌ Difficult to test.

❌ Difficult to reuse.

❌ Large component.

❌ Tight coupling.

❌ Hidden business logic.

---

## Violated Principles

Single Responsibility Principle.

Separation of Concerns.

Reusable Components.

Maintainability.

---

## Better Solution

Split into:

ProductCard

ProductService

CartService

CheckoutService

ReviewService
