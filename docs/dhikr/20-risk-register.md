# 20 — Risk Register

| ID | Risk | Owner | Severity | Likelihood | Mitigation | Release-blocking condition |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| R-01 | Unreviewed or inaccurate religious content is published (source, translation, or grading error) | Scholarly reviewer (per [03](03-authenticity-and-scholarly-review-policy.md)) | Critical | Medium (without gating), Low (with gating enforced) | `reviewStatus` field gates every query path per [12](12-sanity-integration-plan.md); no "publish with caveat" state | **Blocking**: content-gating test from [17](17-test-and-validation-plan.md) must pass before any release |
| R-02 | Scholarly review becomes a bottleneck, stalling v1 launch | Project owner | Medium | Medium | Editorial (Phase 2) runs in parallel with engineering (Phases 1/3/4/5) per [19-implementation-roadmap.md](19-implementation-roadmap.md), so engineering isn't blocked on review completion | Not release-blocking in itself, but Phase 6 cannot start without at least one fully `approved` item |
| R-03 | Audio recitation contains an error not caught by text review (audio ≠ text accuracy) | Scholarly reviewer + technical QA (per [10](10-audio-review-and-delivery.md)) | High | Low-Medium | Audio review is a distinct stage from text review, not assumed correct by association; verified by the audio-text consistency check in [17-test-and-validation-plan.md](17-test-and-validation-plan.md) | **Blocking** for audio specifically: an item's audio cannot go live ahead of its own audio-review clearance |
| R-04 | Arabic text renders incorrectly (missing diacritics, wrong direction, font fallback to unsupported glyphs) | Engineering | Medium | Medium | Rendering requirements in [09-arabic-content-presentation.md](09-arabic-content-presentation.md); real Unicode text only, no images-of-text; verified by the Arabic rendering test category in [17-test-and-validation-plan.md](17-test-and-validation-plan.md) | Blocking for Phase 3 sign-off, not for architecture phase |
| R-05 | Local-storage-only progress data is lost (cache clear, device change) causing visitor frustration | Product owner | Low | High (accepted tradeoff) | Explicitly documented as an accepted v1 tradeoff in [16-privacy-and-local-storage-policy.md](16-privacy-and-local-storage-policy.md); not treated as a defect | Not release-blocking — accepted by design |
| R-06 | Feature scope creeps toward gamification (streaks, badges, leaderboards) contrary to product intent | Product owner | Low | Medium | Explicit non-goals recorded in [01](01-product-scope.md), [07](07-repeat-counter-specification.md), [08](08-memorisation-system.md); flagged here so future scope changes are a conscious decision, not drift | Not release-blocking; a scope-change discussion, not a launch gate |
| R-07 | Accessibility gaps for Arabic/RTL, counter, or audio ship unnoticed (no existing a11y precedent to inherit from — see [15](15-accessibility-requirements.md)) | Engineering | High | Medium | Dedicated accessibility requirements doc plus release-blocking test category per [17](17-test-and-validation-plan.md) | **Blocking**: accessibility test pass required before Phase 3 sign-off |

## Notes on scope

This register covers risks specific to the Dhikr feature as scoped in this pack. General repository risks (hosting, deployment, unrelated department work) are out of scope — see [00-existing-system-audit.md](00-existing-system-audit.md) for what this pack does and doesn't touch.
