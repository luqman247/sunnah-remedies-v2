# §1 — Governing principles

§1.1 — **Knowledge before commerce.** No product launch precedes its monograph and Knowledge Library record. No journey departure precedes its programme page and preparation timeline.

§1.2 — **One voice.** Correspondence reads as the institution, not as marketing. No urgency language. No scarcity. Dates are stated; pressure is not applied.

§1.3 — **Seasonal rhythm follows the tradition and the harvest**, not retail quarters. Ramadan is for reading. Shawwāl for considered Umrah departure. Spring for the olive. Autumn for the desert and the Academy year.

§1.4 — **Amendment.** Postponement is published plainly. A journey deferred for safety receives a correspondence letter, not silence.

§1.5 — **Photography.** Every launch and departure follows `Image-Acquisition-Plan.md` asset IDs before publication.
