# Architecture 06 — Roadmap

## Purpose

Records the phased delivery plan (Phases A–J) at architectural altitude. Detailed milestone breakdowns live in `20-plan/planning/`.

## Scope

The sequence and intent of platform phases, not their task-level detail.

---

# Phased Delivery (A–J)

The platform is delivered in ten sequential phases. Each phase composes the primitives established earlier and never revisits fixed foundations.

| Phase | Focus |
|---|---|
| A | Design-system foundation (fixed primitives, five-typeface stack) |
| B | Core content model and CMS |
| C | Marketing and editorial surfaces |
| D | Apothecary (commerce) |
| E | Academy (courses, lessons) |
| F | Practitioner and clinical surfaces |
| G | Search, filter, and discovery |
| H | Operational backbone (Inngest, Cal.com, Resend, Loops, CRM) |
| I | Portals — Student, Practitioner, and Alumni (first-class) |
| J | Research and editorial arm, hardening |

---

# Sequencing Principles

- A phase never alters a fixed foundation from an earlier phase; it composes it.
- Compliance scope (UK GDPR, EU GDPR, KSA PDPL) is designed in at the phase that introduces the relevant data, not retrofitted.
- Each phase ends with verification against the Definition of Done before the next begins.
