# Architecture 01 — Vision

## Purpose

Establishes the enduring vision the platform architecture exists to serve. Every downstream decision — system design, content model, folder structure, roadmap — must trace back to this document.

## Scope

The Sunnah Remedies digital platform in full: apothecary, therapeutic practice, academy, clinic, and the research and editorial arm, across the London, Copenhagen, and Riyadh presences.

---

# The Vision

Sunnah Remedies is an institution of Prophetic Medicine expressed through software of institutional quality. The platform is not a storefront with articles attached; it is a body of knowledge, a practice, and a place of learning that happens to transact.

The architecture serves three commitments, in priority order:

1. **Integrity** — clinical accuracy and authenticity of source (including hadith grading) are never subordinate to commercial convenience.
2. **Longevity** — the system is built to be maintained for decades, not quarters.
3. **Excellence** — every surface reflects the care of *iḥsān*: doing a thing beautifully because it is worth doing beautifully.

---

# The Governing Doctrine — Two Ledgers, One Standard

The platform is governed by two ledgers held to a single standard:

- The **Integrity Ledger** governs clinical accuracy, source authenticity, and editorial truth.
- The **Commercial Ledger** governs revenue, conversion, and growth.

**The Integrity Ledger holds veto power over the Commercial Ledger.** No commercial optimisation may proceed if it compromises accuracy or authenticity. This is a non-negotiable architectural constraint, not a guideline, and it is enforced at every stage of the lifecycle.

---

# What This Means Architecturally

- Content and its provenance are first-class data, not marketing copy.
- Authenticity grading is a modelled attribute, not an editorial footnote.
- The design system's integrity (typography, palette, motion) is a fixed foundation, composed but never altered by later work.
