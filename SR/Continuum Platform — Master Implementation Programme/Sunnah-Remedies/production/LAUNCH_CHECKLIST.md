# LAUNCH CHECKLIST

Gate for any public launch (site relaunch, shop, academy enrolment, community). All items must pass; evidence linked in the PR or release record.

**Institutional** ☐ Constitution ratified and cited · ☐ IP/branding per brand manual · ☐ Arabic text integrity verified (glyph-accurate rendering, no broken *tashkīl*) · ☐ scholarly attributions verified against sources.
**Standards** ☐ Institutional review checklist passed in full · ☐ accessibility spec conformance · ☐ SEO manual conformance · ☐ bilingual/i18n guide conformance where applicable.
**Commerce (if in scope)** ☐ all 13 commerce-spec acceptance checklists green · ☐ Stripe webhooks idempotent under replay · ☐ refund path tested · ☐ VAT/receipts correct.
**Safety & governance** ☐ Two Ledgers, One Standard verified: no clinical, religious, or above-threshold financial decision is automated · ☐ safeguarding chapter obligations met for any minor-facing surface · ☐ GDPR records of processing updated; special-category data (clinical) excluded until AP-4 platform exists.
**Operations** ☐ runbooks in handbook updated · ☐ monitoring/alerts per Phase 8 spec · ☐ rollback rehearsed.
**Sign-off** ☐ Domain owner · ☐ QA · ☐ Founder.
