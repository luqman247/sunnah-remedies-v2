## The five rules

1. Sources of truth are named in `DOCUMENT_REGISTER.md` — one per domain.
2. Supersede, never overwrite; superseded material moves to the `Archive` repository.
3. All planning lives in `/production` — update it rather than creating new planning documents.
4. Platform-generic knowledge goes upstream to `Continuum` (`ECOSYSTEM.md` §contract).
5. Generated files (marked in the register) are never hand-edited.

## Mandatory vs reference

**Binding:** the constitution; `30-digital-estate/standards/`; the academy framework and standards; brand manual and design system for anything public-facing.
**Reference:** phase specifications (per task), handbook chapters (per role), audits (evidence).
