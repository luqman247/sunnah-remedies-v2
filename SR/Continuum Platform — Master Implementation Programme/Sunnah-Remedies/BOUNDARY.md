# REPOSITORY BOUNDARY — Sunnah-Remedies

**Purpose** — The complete institutional knowledge of Sunnah Remedies: identity, law, teaching, clinical operations, digital products, and business operations.

**Mission** — Enable any employee, practitioner, instructor, executive, or AI agent to understand and operate the institution from this repository alone, for the next twenty years.

**Scope** — Institution constitution and governance · operations handbook · brand and design language (as applied brand) · academy framework, standards, certification, and courses · clinical and Hijama practice material · website, e-commerce, operations, intelligence, and community specifications · marketing, legal, and research material · Sunnah-Remedies-specific AI assistant configuration · the production command centre.

**Non-scope** — Software engineering methodology (→ `Continuum/10-methodology`) · runtime platform architecture, RFCs, and specifications (→ `Continuum/20-platform`) · institution-agnostic digital-institution standards and the starter platform (→ `Continuum/30-dios`) · superseded documents (→ `Archive`).

**Responsibilities** — Ratify and maintain institutional law; define what the institution needs from its platform (in brand-agnostic terms when contributing upstream); own all content, courses, and brand assets; run the production board.

**Consumers** — Founders and executives · academy staff and students · clinical practitioners · developers and designers building the digital estate · operations staff · governed AI agents.

**Outputs** — The live website and shop · academy courses and certifications · clinic operations · knowledge base · community and alumni network.

**Dependencies** — `Continuum` (methodology, platform, DIOS handbook — version pinned per `ECOSYSTEM.md` §4) · `Archive` (provenance).

**Ownership** — Office of the Founders; domain owners per `DOCUMENT_REGISTER.md`.

**Lifecycle** — Living. Constitutions amend by declared procedure; standards by domain-owner review; the production board updates continuously.

**Future growth** — New courses under `20-academy/courses/` · clinical platform and booking specifications under `30-digital-estate/` (currently a declared gap — see `production/PRODUCTION_BOARD.md`) · marketing and research domains added as content arrives, by decision-log entry.
