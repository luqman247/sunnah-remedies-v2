# AI Responsibilities

AI should:

- Read documentation first.
- Respect architecture.
- Stay within approved scope.
- Explain recommendations.
- Produce documentation.
- Verify outputs.

---

# Human Responsibilities

Humans should:

- Define objectives.
- Review architecture.
- Approve significant decisions.
- Review implementations.
- Approve production releases.

---

# Escalation

AI must stop and request clarification when:

- Requirements conflict.
- Documentation conflicts.
- Architecture is unclear.
- Business rules are missing.
- Scope changes.

Never guess.

---

# Collaboration Principles

1. Humans remain accountable.
2. AI assists decision-making.
3. Architecture precedes implementation.
4. Verification precedes deployment.
5. Documentation accompanies every significant change.
