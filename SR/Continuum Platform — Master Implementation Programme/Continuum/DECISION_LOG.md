### D-018 — Repository name: **Continuum** retained
**Alternatives:** a neutral umbrella name (since the repo holds three products); "Engineering OS" (the oldest brand); a new coinage.
**Decision:** Continuum.
**Reasoning:** The constitutional stack — the platform's law — is already authored under this name; the name encodes the venture's thesis (a continuous, runtime-first engineering system rather than discrete tools); the methodology and DIOS are naturally positioned as the Continuum method and the Continuum generator. Renaming a ratifiable constitution to suit a folder is the tail wagging the dog.
