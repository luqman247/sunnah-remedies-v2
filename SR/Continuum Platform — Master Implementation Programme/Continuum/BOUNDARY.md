# REPOSITORY BOUNDARY — Continuum

**Purpose** — Everything required to design, build, verify, release, and operate software the AI-native way, and to generate governed digital institutions — independent of any one consumer.

**Mission** — Become the platform a serious organisation trusts for twenty years of software delivery; commercially separable at any time.

**Scope** — Engineering methodology (lifecycle, standards, workflows, templates, prompts, CI) · platform architecture, RFCs, and constitutional specifications (runtime, context graph, engines, verification, SDK, security, upgrade) · DIOS: the institution-agnostic operating system, starter-platform specification, implementation programme, and (future) `create-institution` generator · platform templates and engineering AI workflows.

**Non-scope** — Any Sunnah Remedies content: brand, courses, clinical material, website copy, commerce configuration, institutional governance (→ `Sunnah-Remedies`) · superseded material (→ `Archive`) · implementation codebases (live in product repos generated/governed by this one).

**Responsibilities** — Keep the methodology coherent and self-contained · complete and maintain the constitutional specification stack · version DIOS and publish upgrade notes · accept consumer requirements only in brand-agnostic form.

**Consumers** — Sunnah Remedies (first and reference instantiation) · future institutions generated via DIOS · engineering teams and AI agents adopting the methodology.

**Outputs** — Versioned DIOS releases · the specification stack · methodology releases · (future) generated institution platforms.

**Dependencies** — None. This repository must remain meaningful if every consumer disappeared. (`Archive` for provenance only.)

**Ownership** — Platform Engineering; product owners per folder (`10` methodology, `20` platform, `30` DIOS).

**Lifecycle** — Living. Constitutional documents amend by their declared procedures; RFC set v1.0 is stable reference; the specification programme is the active workstream.

**Future growth** — Completion of specifications 001–015 · runtime implementation · the `create-institution` generator (DIOS Phase 9) · possible extraction of products into separate repositories **only when** they gain separate owners or release cadences (D-015 records why not yet).
