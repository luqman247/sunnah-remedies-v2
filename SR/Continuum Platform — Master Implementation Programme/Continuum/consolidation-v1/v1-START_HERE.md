## What is this repository?

The consolidated, canonical knowledge base of Sunnah Remedies: the institution, its academy, its digital estate, its engineering method, and its two platform ventures (DIOS and Continuum). It was produced by a full audit and consolidation of eleven prior archives on 6 July 2026. It supersedes all of them.

## How is it organised?

By knowledge domain, in numbered folders. Lower numbers carry higher institutional authority:

```
00-institution/     Who we are and how we govern           ← highest authority
10-brand/           How we look, sound, and photograph
20-academy/         How we teach, assess, and certify
30-digital-estate/  What the website and products must be
40-engineering/     How software is designed, built, verified, released
50-dios/            The reusable Digital Institution Operating System
60-continuum/       The Continuum AI-native platform venture
90-templates/       Index of reusable templates
archive/            Everything superseded — history, never deleted
```

Root files (`README.md`, this file, `KNOWLEDGE_MAP.md`, `KNOWLEDGE_GRAPH.md`, `DOCUMENT_REGISTER.md`, `DECISION_LOG.md`, `CHANGELOG.md`, `IMPLEMENTATION_ROADMAP.md`) govern the repository itself.

## Which documents are mandatory?

The constitutions (`00-institution/constitution/`, `50-dios/operating-system/00 Institution Constitution.md`, `60-continuum/constitutional-specifications/000-platform-constitution.md`) bind everything beneath them in their domains. The standards folders (`30-digital-estate/standards/`, `50-dios/operating-system/`) are mandatory for any work in their scope. Everything else is reference, specification, or template — consult as your task requires.

## Which documents are generated — never edit by hand?

- `50-dios/implementation-programme/generated-docx/` — styled Word renderings of the Markdown sources beside them.
- `60-continuum/technical-specification-v1/CONSOLIDATED.md` — single-file concatenation of the RFC set.
- `10-brand/assets/` logo exports — regenerate from the SVG masters.
- `DOCUMENT_REGISTER.md` — regenerated by script when the repository changes.

## Where does new documentation go?

Into the domain folder it belongs to, in `kebab-case`, with a register entry and a changelog line. If no domain fits, the taxonomy is wrong — record a decision in `DECISION_LOG.md` before inventing a folder.

## How does documentation evolve?

Supersede, never overwrite. The old document moves to `/archive`, the new one takes its place, `DECISION_LOG.md` records why, `CHANGELOG.md` records what. The archive is append-only.

## How do I find anything?

- `DOCUMENT_REGISTER.md` — every document, its purpose, audience, type, and status.
- `KNOWLEDGE_MAP.md` — the guided tour, folder by folder.
- `KNOWLEDGE_GRAPH.md` — dependencies and reading order between documents.
