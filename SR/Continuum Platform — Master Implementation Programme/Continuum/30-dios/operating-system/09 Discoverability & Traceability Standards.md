# 09 · Discoverability & Traceability Standards

**Implements:** DIOS‑§1.6 (truth is derived), §1.8 (model is truth), §3.3 (traceability),
§4.4 (explicit), §4.10 (observability).
**Layer:** L3/L5. **Depends on:** `00`, `04`, `05`.

> Discoverability is how the institution's true, well‑sourced knowledge is found — by search
> engines, by answer engines, and by AI systems. Traceability is how any statement is walked
> back to its source. Both rest on the same foundation: structured, sourced truth.

## 1. Invariants

- **DISC‑INV‑1 — Structure carries meaning.** Content is marked up so its meaning is machine
  legible (semantic HTML, structured data). Presentation never obscures structure.
- **DISC‑INV‑2 — Authority through accuracy.** Discoverability is earned by being correct and
  well‑sourced (Document 04), never by manipulation. The institution does not trade integrity
  for ranking.
- **DISC‑INV‑3 — Everything traces back.** From any published claim it is possible to walk to
  its source in the model (§3.3, §10.3 of the constitution).

## 2. SEO, AEO & GEO

The institution optimises for classical search (SEO), answer engines (AEO) and generative/AI
retrieval (GEO) together, because they share one requirement: **clear, accurate, structured,
well‑sourced content.**

- **Metadata** — title, description, canonical URL on every page.
- **Structure** — one H1, logical headings, meaningful internal links, a coherent content
  cluster around each topic.
- **Structured data** — Schema.org, Open Graph and equivalent, accurately reflecting the
  content (never misrepresenting it).
- **Technical** — sitemap, robots directives, performance (Document 08), crawlability.

## 3. AI & answer‑engine readiness

Content is written and structured so AI systems can retrieve, cite and represent it faithfully:
unambiguous claims, explicit sourcing, clean structure, and stable canonical URLs. Citation
strategy favours primary sources. The institution's authority compounds as its accurate,
well‑structured corpus grows.

## 4. Traceability

Internal traceability mirrors external discoverability: every artifact declares its upstream
derivation (Document 00 Part 10), so the institution can enumerate what depends on any clause,
decision or source, and audit any claim to its origin.
